Dolls Island, Mexico

How about taking a trip to Mexico and getting to know the famous (and hideous) Ilha das Bonecas? Xochimilco has an extensive network of channels that are great for sailing - one of the routes ends at a place that has trees with pieces of plastic dolls all over it. Despite the frightening appearance, the deformed and rotten dolls are a form of homage to a girl who drowned on the beach while playing with a doll.
